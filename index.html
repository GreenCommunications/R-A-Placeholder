Synergy NEW
Synergy aspires to specialize in advancing hydrogen vehicles to markets other than ones with hydrogen infrastructure. Hydrogen is the future in automotive technology because of its abundance and simplicity. Hydrogen fuel cell vehicles exist and are doing great in experimental communities such as California. The prevailing complications with this technology is a lack of infrastructure outside of these small test communities. Our solution to this colossal problem is a vehicle that produces hydrogen onboard the vehicle, this in turn eliminates the need of hydrogen infrastructure. We are exceptionally committed to bringing the future closer than ever before.

Synergy is an automotive startup based out of Columbus Ohio that is centralizing the future not tommorow, but today.

Synergy aspires to expand the hydrogen and automotive industry in ways the industry has never seen before.

With a dedicated team, Synergy is ready to bring the future into your hands.
